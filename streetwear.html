Urban Culture and It's Major Impact on the Market of Streetwear

How is Designer Streetwear Made?

Making and designing streetwear is abstract, there is no real answer on how to make any piece of clothing and it can be up to your ideas and beliefs on how to create it. However, there is a trend that many artist follow. Many of the pieces are trendy and follow urban culture. If you enjoy surfing or music you would probably want a piece of clothing that would resemble your interests. Despite this, the main trend that artists follow is popularity and exposure. They try and get many popular celebrities and influences to wear clothing to increase the amount of people that view their work. Typically when your favorite musician or movie star is wearing a certain piece of clothing or style many people who look up to these individuals will more than likely purchase these pieces. Many designer streetwear pieces are made in certain ways such as being cutoff to show belts, and are usually made of premium materials along with casual fashion and luxury items, The history behind the clothing has much more meaning behind the actual manufacturing of these casual pieces of clothing because anybody can create a piece of clothing with premium materials. The influence of celebrities and those who embrace the clothing are those that really attract and bring the revenue and kickstart brands.

How has streetwear impacted fashion?

Streetwear has been around for a long time however, many were considered the original founders of streetwear for example Shawn Stussy founder of Stussy brand laid the foundation for many styles of streetwear and started a generation of clothing before it was even considered. Today many of his styles are still represented in clothing and his influence on the structure of clothing created a strong foundation for the future. We can see artists such as Drake, Travis Scott and other notable rappers start their own clothing brands with their own branding and merchandise for sale using similar styles to Stussy with their own twist. However their popularity has allowed for their brand to grow at a tremendously large rate.

Who wears streetwear?

A variety of individuals from hip-hop artists, skaters, surfers, influencers, and many others including people in your class can be seen wearing casual streetwear. Recently streetwear has seen a major increase due to K-pop or korean pop music which has rapidly grown in popularity has started to spread a new wave across the world for style and those who closely follow the trending culture. The use of social media has greatly influence the pop culture and shown what is trending leading to a popularity and high demand in fashion pieces. It is used to represent a persons unique style as well as characteristic and each piece of clothing has its own meaning for each individual and can really represent them as a person.
