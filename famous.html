Celebrities are the power to marketing in Fashion

Impact of Celebrities in Fashion

Celebrities have a large influence in marketing for fashion. You can see them show up to concerts in a wide variety of outfits, or even out in public with crazy styles. The same can be said for sports players, you can see many show up with flashy styles, sleek and minimalistic designs and casual clothing. Each persons style is unique however it is up to the viewer to perceive what they see. If you have a strong liking towards a favorite sports team, or genre of music or enjoy a certain activity it bases you outfits and clothing of choice. However in the influencers at the top of each category and hobby depict what is being worn. Most of the time others decide to follow the trend and tail the same kind of outfit choices and clothing brands that their favorite stars like to wear which is why the impact of Celebrities in fashion is so impactful.
