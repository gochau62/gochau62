What is Streetwear and Designer Clothing? The New Influence on Urban Culture.

What Is Considered Streetwear and Designer

Luxury streetwear can be defined as a collection of clothing that has not only been influenced by todays standards and youth culture, it has also influenced many popular labels like Gucci and Louis Vuitton. Streetwear and designer clothing have became a staple and icon that has defined the luxury market of clothing. These luxurious brands highlight culture as well as defining your social status in addition to personal income and values on luxury clothing.

Defining Designer

Designer clothing can also be referred to as expensive luxury clothing that is made of high quality materials and designed by a famous fashion designer. These clothes are designed by professions who seek to create clothing that incorporates functionality as well as design in addition to clothing that is aesthetically pleasing and follows todays trends. Many of the clothing worn today can be used to represent social status as many ot those who tend to wear more expensive clothing can be shown by wearing clothing with higher quality materials, name brands, and many other additions that make it "Designer"

Defining Streetwear

Streetwear is defined as a simple style of clothing that is fashionable and includes influences of urban culture which reside within a subculture group such as skaters and new york "drillers". It is not only confined to these groups but a wide and diverse group of individuals that come together to shape and influence the urban youth subgroups. Streetwear can also be defined as the production, promotion, and resale of casual fashion making the retail prices high and stock low which makes finding shoes and shirts of designer streetwear that much harder. These brands often try to target younger urban audiences such as those under 25 years old as many of the pieces are shown to have a stronger influence in teens to mid adults who want casual fashion but with a limited price tag and value to them.
